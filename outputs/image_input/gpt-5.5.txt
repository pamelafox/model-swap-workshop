=== Is this a unicorn? (examples/image_aurochs.jpg) ===
No. It has two curved horns, so it isn’t a unicorn. It looks more like a bovine—possibly an ox, bull, or an aurochs-style illustration.

=== Are these alligators or crocodiles? (examples/image_crocodile.png) ===
They look **more like alligators** than crocodiles.

The main clues are their **broader, more U-shaped snouts** and generally darker, bulkier appearance. Crocodiles usually have **narrower, V-shaped snouts** and often show more prominent teeth from the side. From this overhead view I can’t be 100% certain, but these appear to be **young alligators**.
